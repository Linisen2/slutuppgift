Commission Information

Terms of Service:

  • You are not to use my artwork for ANY merchandise at all, that includes non-profit merch, without my express permission!
  • I ask that you credit me when using my artwork on any platform by either placing a panel I offer you in your twitch panels, a command, or honestly any other way. Just something that lets your viewers know the original artist for the artwork. If you are truly against it, I get it, but I ask that you consider the time and effort I put into working on your pieces.
  • Any commissioned artworks are for your personal use only. The files are not to be distributed or given to anyone without my permission, that includes other graphic artists for panels, overlays, etc.
  • I maintain all rights to use any artwork I make, that includes commissions, meaning that I may use it for my own personal uses, however I will never resell any piece commissioned from me whatsoever. You have my guarantee!
  • My work is not to be distributed, copied, or altered in ANY way without my permission!
  • If you bully/rush me and/or are overall rude, disrespectful, or break the terms I will turn down/terminate the commission. I work very hard and I am only human so I need time to wind down. I can't work 24/7.
  • If you commission me this means that you agree to all of my terms and conditions and will follow them.

Payment:

  • Payment is made through PayPal. You will receive and invoice through PayPal. If you do not have a PayPal account please let me before hand as I will send you a link to the invoice.
  • At least half of the payment is due before hand as a deposit on all works (excluding sketches) done so once the details are worked through about what you'd like I will walk you through how to pay and your total.
  • All major changes to the emote/sketch ideas have to be done in the sketching phase, once I begin to finalize the pieces I will only allow minor changes or fees to cover major ones and the redraw.
  • Prices are NON-NEGOTIABLE and I will not be bullied into lowering them. I work incredibly hard to deliver quality art and I feel that my prices are very fair for my experience and background.
  • Payments are NONREFUNDABLE once I start working on your commission. If you cancel the commission before I start finalizing you will recieve a 50% refund. Once I complete the commission there will be NO refunds! This is non-negotiable so please be sure you can afford the commission before the commission starts.

What I Won't Draw:

  • Mechs/Mecha
  • Nudity
  • Over-complicated Armor
  • Copies of someone else's art (I will take references, but I will not copy a piece)
