About me

My name is Lina, but some of you might know me as Linisen. I am an aspiring self taught artist who dreams about becoming a full time commission artist and twitch streamer. On my Twitch stream I am a creative and variety game streamer. During streams I mostly do illustrations, but I also do full channel art, Twitch emotes and playing different games that I like. Along with many more things!

I started doing digital art seriously at the end of 2016 after I had gotten my first drawing tablet. After some months of drawing on and off I realized this was my passion and that I want to draw for a living. To spread my creativity and make people happy with my art.

I hope to see you in my Twitch stream.
