Rush Jobs

  • Rush jobs are available at any time unless stated otherwise.
  • To rush a job it will be your order total plus 50%.
  • Ex. If you want to rush one emote it would be $18+ $9.
  • You can choose to rush only part of your order.
  • Rush jobs are done as soon as possible. If there are people ahead of you I will give you an estimate on when yours would be likely to start, however be aware that this date can change (usually moved up).
